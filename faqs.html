Frequently Asked Questions

What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG developed by HoYoverse. The game features a massive, gorgeous map called Teyvat, elemental combat system, and gacha mechanics for obtaining new characters and weapons.

Is Genshin Impact free to play?

Yes, Genshin Impact is free to play. While there are optional in-game purchases, all content can be completed without spending money.

How do I start playing?

You can download Genshin Impact from the official website, mobile app stores, or gaming platforms like PlayStation Store. Create an account and start your adventure!

What platforms is Genshin Impact available on?

Genshin Impact is available on PC, iOS, Android, PlayStation 4, and PlayStation 5, with cross-save functionality between most platforms.
